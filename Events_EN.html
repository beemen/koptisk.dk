Coptic conference in Vienna/Austria during Easter holiday in 2013

The Coptic Orthodox Church in Denmark has arranged a conference in Vienna / Austria during the Easter holiday in 2013. 55 members of the Coptic Orthodox Church in Denmark attended this conference. The conference was hosted by his holiness Bishop Gabriel (The Bishop of the Coptic Orthodox Church in Austria) and under the auspices of his holiness Bishop Abakir (The Bishop of the Coptic Orthodox Church in Scandinavia). Father Mousa Abdalla "The priest of Coptic Orthodox Church in Denmark" also attended the conference.

Information meeting arrangement on 20th January 2013

The Coptic Orthodox Church in Denmark arranged on the 20th January 2013 information meeting. The purpose of this meeting was to present the Coptic Orthodox Church in Denmark to the Danish society, such information about the history of the Coptic Orthodox Church in Denmark as well as in Egypt, information about the Coptic language, information about the Coptic monasteries and Christianity in Egypt. Many guests and representatives attended the meeting from different private and public organizations.
